## 💰 Smart Accounting (Chat-Based)

Manage finances without logging into the system:

**Example (via WhatsApp):**

```
"I paid 20,000 for transportation"
"Bought office supplies for 15,500"
```

➡️ BOSS automatically:

* Parses the message
* Records the expense
* Categorizes it correctly

> No dashboards. No manual entry. Just chat.
